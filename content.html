Controls

Playing BitLife is simple and intuitive:

  • Tap/Click: Select options and make decisions
  • Scroll: Browse through different life events and choices
  • Age Button: Progress one year in your character's life
  • Menu Icons: Access various life aspects (career, relationships, assets, activities)
  • Stats Bar: Monitor your character's happiness, health, smarts, and looks

How to Play

  1. Start Your Life

    • Choose your character's gender and birthplace
    • Get assigned random starting stats and family background

  2. Make Life Choices

    • Age up year by year
    • Make decisions about education
    • Choose career paths
    • Build relationships
    • Manage finances and assets

  3. Key Features

    • Career Development: From fast food worker to CEO
    • Relationships: Date, marry, have children
    • Education: Study hard, attend university
    • Activities: Go to the gym, meditate, party
    • Crime: Choose to follow or break the law
    • Legacy: Continue playing as your children
